\section{Research Questions}
\label{sec:research-questions}
% Enumerate the RQs that structure the thesis.

This thesis addresses the following research questions:

\begin{enumerate}
    \item \textbf{RQ1}: Which self-supervised pre-training objective (I-JEPA, DINOv2, MAE) generalizes best across cities for end-to-end planning, compared to supervised ImageNet features?

    \item \textbf{RQ2}: Which city in the NAVSIM benchmark (Boston, Las Vegas, Pittsburgh, Singapore) serves as the best single-city ``training ground'' for zero-shot transfer?

    \item \textbf{RQ3}: Do SSL-pretrained features degrade more gracefully than supervised baselines under increasing domain shift (cross-city transfer and reduced training data)?

    \item \textbf{RQ4}: Can a camera-only planner combining I-JEPA features with a truncated diffusion planning head match the EPDMS of heavier multi-modal methods?

    \item \textbf{RQ5}: Does pairing SSL features with a generative (diffusion) planning head reduce cross-city generalization gaps compared to SSL with a regression head?

    \item \textbf{RQ6} (exploratory): Do reinforcement learning post-training gains (GRPO/DPO) transfer across cities?
\end{enumerate}

\section{Contributions}
\label{sec:contributions}

\textit{Summarize the concrete contributions:}
\begin{enumerate}
    \item \textbf{NAVSIM City Splits Benchmark}: The first per-city train/test partitioning of NAVSIM, enabling systematic cross-city generalization studies. (Submitted to IROS 2026.)

    \item \textbf{Cross-City SSL Generalization Study}: A comprehensive evaluation of SSL backbones (I-JEPA, DINOv2, MAE) vs.\ supervised ResNet across TransFuser and Latent TransFuser (LAW), revealing that cross-city failure is primarily a \emph{representation} problem, not a planning-architecture problem.

    \item \textbf{JEPA-Diffusion Planner}: A novel camera-only, edge-deployable planner combining frozen I-JEPA ViT-S/14 with truncated diffusion trajectory generation (based on DiffusionDrive), targeting competitive EPDMS with real-time inference.

    \item \textbf{Generative vs.\ Regression Head Analysis}: First comparison of SSL + diffusion head vs.\ SSL + regression head for cross-city transfer in NAVSIM.
\end{enumerate}
